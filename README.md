# Power BI Project – Hospitality Business Insights

##  Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **Power BI dashboard** designed to analyze key performance metrics of a hospitality business. The dashboard enables data-driven decision-making by tracking revenue, occupancy, booking trends, and customer behavior across multiple properties and cities.

The dataset contains information about hotels under the **Atliq brand** across major Indian cities such as Mumbai, Delhi, Hyderabad, and Bangalore. The analysis focuses on identifying trends, performance gaps, and growth opportunities in the hospitality sector.

---

##  Key Metrics & KPIs Analyzed

* **Revenue** (City-wise, Property-wise, Weekly trends)
* **RevPAR (Revenue per Available Room)**
* **Occupancy %**
* **ADR (Average Daily Rate)**
* **DSRN (Daily Sellable Room Nights)**
* **DBRN (Daily Booked Room Nights)**
* **DURN (Daily Utilized Room Nights)**
* **Realisation %**
* **Cancellation %**
* **Average Rating**

---

##  Dashboard Highlights

* **Property Performance** – Compare revenue, occupancy, and ADR across properties.
* **City-Level Insights** – Analyze performance in Delhi, Mumbai, Hyderabad, and Bangalore.
* **Revenue by Category** – Breakdown into **Luxury** vs **Business** segments.
* **Booking Platform Analysis** – Evaluate ADR and realization % across platforms (direct, online, offline, third-party apps).
* **Time-Based Trends** – Weekly revenue, occupancy, and RevPAR analysis.
* **Weekday vs Weekend Trends** – Compare customer booking behavior and revenue generation.

##  Sample Insights

* Mumbai properties generated the **highest revenue** with strong occupancy.
* **Luxury category** contributed ~61% of total revenue, compared to ~39% from business properties.
* **Weekends** showed higher occupancy and RevPAR compared to weekdays.

##  Conclusion

This Power BI project provides a comprehensive overview of the hospitality business performance. It highlights how data visualization can uncover actionable insights, helping businesses optimize revenue, reduce cancellations, and improve customer satisfaction.
